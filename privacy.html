Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

RYST 109A ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at stay.ryst.in and the private beach villa located at Parmankeni, Cheyyur, Tamil Nadu. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website or request a stay, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you submit a booking request through our website, we collect the details you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • WhatsApp number / phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Requested check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests and the occasion for your stay
  • Any special requests or messages you send us

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your booking request and confirm availability;
  • Communicate with you about your stay, including pricing, arrangements and follow-up;
  • Maintain a record of confirmed bookings.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Booking requests & WhatsApp

Our booking form prepares a message and opens WhatsApp so you can send your request to us directly. When you communicate with us over WhatsApp, your message is handled in accordance with WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing it to understand how WhatsApp (Meta) processes your data.

Cookies & analytics

Our website does not set advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not run third-party analytics or advertising scripts. Fonts and interactive scripts used on the site are served from our own domain rather than external providers.

Third-party links

Our website may link to external services such as Instagram, Google Maps and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those services. Please review their respective privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain booking-related information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, manage your stay, and meet any legal or accounting obligations. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have shared with us. To make a request, please contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
